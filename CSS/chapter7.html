Header Area (Fixed Height)

1. Background Origin (Image Position)

No background-origin (padding-box default):

The image starts from the padding edge.

background-origin: border-box:

The image starts from the outer border edge.

background-origin: content-box:

The image starts from the content area.

2. Background Clip (Color Painting Area)

No background-clip (border-box default):

Color extends under the border area.

background-clip: padding-box:

Color stops at the padding edge.

background-clip: content-box:

Color is only visible behind the text content.

3. CSS Fixed Position

Look at the red circle at the bottom right. It stays fixed while scrolling.
