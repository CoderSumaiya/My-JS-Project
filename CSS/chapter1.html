Section 1: Understanding EM Units (Relative)

Fixed 20px: This font is a fixed 20 pixels

1.5em: It is 1.5 times the browser's default font size

I am Parent (15px)
I am Child (15px * 2em = 30px)
I am Grandchild (30px * 2em = 60px) — Oh no! Growing bigger!

The 'em' unit scales proportionally based on its parent's font size
